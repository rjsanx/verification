## 🏛️ Current Role & Organization

### National Informatics Centre (NIC)
The National Informatics Centre is the premier science and technology organization of the Government of India in the field of e-Governance. NIC provides network backbone, e-Governance support, and IT consultancy to the Central Government, State Governments, UT Administrations, Districts, and other government bodies.

**Official Website**: [https://www.nic.in/](https://www.nic.in/)  
**Parent Ministry**: Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY)

### Directorate of Land Records and Survey (DLRS)
DLRS is responsible for maintenance and updating of land records in West Bengal, including the BanglarBhumi portal - the state's official land records management system.

**Public Portal**: [https://banglarbhumi.gov.in/](https://banglarbhumi.gov.in/)  
**Parent Department**: Land & Land Reforms Department, Government of West Bengal
